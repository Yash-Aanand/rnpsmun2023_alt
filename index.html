WHAT IS R-NPSMUN

NPS Rajajinagar proudly presents the sixth edition of R-NPSMUN!

An amalgamation of consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented.

Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will help sail you through! So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!

Secretary General of RNPSMUN 2022

Dhruti Deshpande!


“Only a few know, how much one must know to know how little he knows” -Werner Heisenberg
Hardworking, determined, and deeply insightful, Dhruti is usually found engaging animatedly in spirited conversations. With her love for facts and greater love for rhetoric, she’ll make sure that she’s one step ahead of the rest with her methodical strategizing and impeccable research, and will certainly leave you rethinking everything you said even if she cannot convince you. She also feels that most of the world’s problems can be solved by asking the right questions and having a logical approach. When not swimming or MUNing, you might find her reading a captivating book on Indian history (or just rereading Harry Potter!) or researching the latest scientific developments. An amiable and friendly person, she’ll certainly get along with you as long as you have an aversion for Comic Sans too, and will make sure that you have an exhilarating and fun-filled conference!

    LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

    Greetings Delegates!

        On behalf of the RNPSMUN 2022 family, I am honored to extend my warmest welcome to each and every one of you to the 6th edition of RNPSMUN. Having had our last two editions online, an offline edition is a long-awaited change. This MUN seeks to bring out the best in each delegate, in terms of both diplomacy and leadership, and I can assure you that by the end of these three days, not only would you have learnt a lot more than you expected, but also grown into a more confident and assertive person. The rigorous efforts which have gone in for the past numerous weeks reflect in what we all strive for – a glorious success of this conference. With five engaging committees, each with exciting agendas, a professional Executive Board, an enthusiastic Organizing Committee and a dedicated Secretariat, we promise you an educational and fun-filled conference, along with a great learning experience and lots of gratifying memories.

    From being a nervous first timer a few years ago to being the Secretary General of RMUN 2022, the journey has been phenomenal, and I truly hope you find MUNing as delightful as I do. Regardless of whether you win or not, it is the impact you make that matters most. As future global citizens, it is our responsibility to find solutions for the various pressing issues that plague the world today, and change, after all, starts with small conversations among people who care. This conference is a small step that will go a long way. Please feel free to reach out to me in case of any queries.

    Looking forward to meeting you all!

    -Dhruti Deshpande

Director General of RNPSMUN 2022

Vishal Rao


Competitive, driven, and fun-loving, there's nothing Vishal finds more interesting than the unknown. With his quick wit and ability to think on his feet, Vishal tends to gravitate towards chaos, then breaks it down with razor sharp reasoning and deduction to arrive at the most logical outcome. But the real fun for Vishal lies in not knowing what comes next, and having to rely only on yourself to come out on top no matter what the situation. His position as School Prefect is a testament to his friendly and approachable nature as well as his leadership qualities. When he isn't actively involved in achieving world domination, he can often be found playing piano, basketball or watching anime. With Vishal serving as this RNPS-MUN's Director General, you can be assured of an incredible and electrifying conference. So, veterans and first timers alike, get ready for three days of fast-paced and stimulating debate that will truly test your limits and push the boundaries of the standard for a high school debate.

    LETTER FROM THE DIRECTOR GENERAL

    Greetings, Delegates!

        It is my honour and privilege to welcome every one of you to the sixth installment of RNPS MUN. As is tradition, we plan to raise the standard yet again, and make this year’s MUN the best we’ve held so far. To write off this MUN as just another inter-school event would not be doing it justice. Our Secretariat and EB have worked tirelessly to make every moment of this MUN an incredible experience. With five amazing committees, you really can’t make a wrong choice. Veteran debater or first timer, you’re guaranteed to find a committee for you. In accordance with our past standards, RNPS MUN will not let you down.

    In the present state of the world, the importance of MUN is more apparent than ever. The need to speak out and make your opinion heard is ever-growing in this environment of uncertainty and turmoil. A MUN is a loudspeaker for your voice, and breathes new life into your words. Winning is important, yes. But the true worth of a MUN lies in the memories and knowledge you walk away with. So, dive headfirst into three days of rigorous debate, deliberation, press conferences and crises. The future is in our hands, and it’s time to prove ourselves worthy of that mantle. And as always, may the best delegate win.

    -Vishal Rao
