Indian Constituent Assembly of 1948

“I have come to this conclusion that there is only one country in the world which understands religion - It is India”

-Swami Vivekananda

The driving force behind the glory of the nation we call Bharat is an idea. An idea that has existed for thousands of years, allowing countless people to live the way they see fit, without fear. That idea, is tolerance. But as the years have passed and the patience of our people has run dry, a dark cloud looms over the future of this country. So RNPS MUN calls out to you, delegates, to answer the burning questions: Is our nation truly tolerant of all religions? Can we confidently say that we, as a country, accept all people as they are irrespective of their faiths and beliefs? And above all, how are we to progress as a nation amidst this environment of animosity and prejudice?
