International Press Corps

“The First Law of Journalism: to confirm existing prejudice, rather than contradict it”

-Alexander Cockburn

The international press committee is designed to represent journalists and the media. The IPC's agenda for its journalists in this committee, is to tackle political polarization in relation to modern journalism. It is an issue prevalent in the modern day and we aim to try and find a ethical solution to this problem by covering the topic of censorship and bias.
